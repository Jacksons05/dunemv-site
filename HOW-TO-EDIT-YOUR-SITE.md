# Editing Your Dune Website — The Easy Guide

You can update your own website whenever you like — change the words, swap a photo,
add or remove a brand, write a journal post. When you're happy, you send it to
Islandmark and we make it live. **You can't break anything.**

---

## Your 3 steps, every time

1. **Change it** — type your edit in the box.
2. **Save** — click the Save button.
3. **Send it to us** — click "In review." That's it. We publish it for you.

Everything below is just extra detail. Those three steps are the whole job.

---

## Getting in

**The first time:** we'll email you an invite. Open it, click **Accept**, and pick
a password you'll remember. That sets up your account.

**Every time after:** go to **your editor link** and log in with your email and
that password. *(Bookmark the page so it's easy to find.)*

---

## What you can change

- **Words** — your tagline, hours, phone, email, the writing on your pages.
- **Brands** — add a brand, remove one, or edit a brand's note. (You'll use this most.)
- **Journal** — write a new post or update an old one.
- **Photos** — swap the storefront, the gallery, or the Instagram pictures.
- **Store policies** — the returns / shipping / privacy text at the bottom.

When you add a photo, please fill in the little **"photo description"** box. It
takes five seconds and keeps your site easy for everyone to use.

---

## Good to know

- **You can't break the live site.** Every change waits in line until we publish it.
  So feel free to try things.
- **There's no "publish" button for you** — that's on purpose. You send; we publish.
- **Want a bigger change?** New section, new page, a design tweak — that part's us.
  Just call or text.
